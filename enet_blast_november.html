Mass Innovation Nights 128 (MIN)

Title: Mass Innovation Nights #126
Date: 2019 November 20
Time: 6:00 PM - 8:30 PM
Location: UMass Lowell's Innovation Hub, 110 Canal Street, Lowell, MA
Price: Free, RSVP Requested

Description: Design-for-usability is a critical aspect of medical device product development. More than a third of medical device incidents involve a use error and more than half of all device recalls for design problems involve the user interface. Because of this, the FDA has made medical device usability a key topic of concern and has issued guidance. User experience plays a vital role throughout the development process from early feasibility to validation to prevent these user errors from occurring.
